Our Forensics Tools

The "screenshare experience" is delivered through interactive demos, video tutorials, and detailed documentation in each tool's GitHub repository.

RitzyScans

RitzyScans is a professional-grade registry scanner targeting MUICache, Store, BAM, DAM, and more. It extracts paths from processes and services, analyzes jumplists, and reports on service statuses. Results are exported to CSV for further analysis.

Download RitzyScans

Timeline Explorer (by Eric Zimmerman)

Analyze RitzyScans' CSV exports with Eric Zimmerman's Timeline Explorer. This tool offers advanced filtering, grouping, and visualization for forensic timelines.

Download Timeline Explorer
